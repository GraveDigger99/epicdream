About EpicDream

EpicDream is a store selling in-game services in World of Warcraft. The store staff provide boosting services for your character. The order is fulfilled by transferring the account for a while to a company employee (booster) or by playing along with the booster: increasing the rating, fulfilling certain conditions, winning, etc.

A team with experience since the release of World of Warcraft: Legion (2016). We are guided by the following princes:

  • Saving clients time, buying a product you save time - while you sleep we work;
  • Money back guarantee in case of dissatisfaction with the execution of the order;
  • We consult for free on the character and on the choice of service;
  • Execution of the order on time and in accordance with the wishes of the clients;
  • We do not change your in-game settings.
